Description:
====================================
Bubble Shooter is a fun and simple puzzle game where you pop bubbles by matching three or more of the same color.

====================================
Features:
====================================
- Simple mouse controls for aiming and shooting.
- Vibrant and engaging visuals.
- Tracks and displays your high score.

====================================
How to Play:
====================================
1. Aim: Move your mouse to aim the shooter.
2. Shoot: Click to shoot the bubble towards the target.
3. Match: Connect 3 or more bubbles of the same color to pop them.
4. Clear: Clear all bubbles to win and advance to the next level.

====================================
Installation:
====================================
1. Clone the Repository:
	$ git clone https://github.com/yousifpa98/bubble-shooter.git

2. Open "index.html" in your browser
